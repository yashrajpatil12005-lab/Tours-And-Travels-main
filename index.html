❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about safari bookings, travel planning, or accommodations? Find answers below.

How do I book a Tadoba safari slot?

+

You can fill out our website booking form with your contact details, government ID proof (Aadhar/PAN), and package selection. Once submitted, our team will verify real-time safari gate availability and contact you via phone or WhatsApp to finalize slot details and payments.

What is included in the Tadoba Jungle Safari Package?

+

Our standard package includes 2D/1N accommodation near the safari gates, all major meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner), one open Gypsy jungle safari ticket with expert guide fees, and pick-up/drop-off transport from the nearest station/airport.

Do you organize customized corporate or group tours?

+

Yes, absolutely! We specialize in organizing custom group tours, family getaways, and corporate retreats to all destinations including Lonavala, Malvan, Mahabaleshwar, and Tadoba. Select the "Custom Package Enquiry" option in the form and add your requirements in the special requests area.

What are the rules regarding government ID proofs?

+

For safari bookings, the government forest department strictly requires a valid ID number (like Aadhar Card, PAN Card, or Passport) for all passengers. The same physical ID must be carried during the safari entry check. All bookings are non-transferable once registered with the forest department.
